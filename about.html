About Us

We here in Spirit Farer aim to provide the living the freedom to choose their own or their loved one's ceremony. Death is a concept beyond anyone's control, how to treat death, is not. We cannot choose when to die, but we can choose how to celebrate it, life and death. We help create ceremonies tailored to the character of the passing.
